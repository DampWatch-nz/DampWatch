Mold & Moisture Remedies – From Simple to Extreme

1. Simple Fixes

  • Wipe down visible moisture or mold with a damp cloth.
  • Keep windows open or use extractor fans to improve ventilation.
  • Use dehumidifiers to maintain indoor humidity below 40%.
  • Fix small leaks or drips in taps, pipes, or roofs immediately.
  • Move furniture slightly away from walls to allow airflow.

2. Moderate Actions

  • Indoor humidity below 50%
  • Apply anti-mold sprays or cleaners to affected surfaces.
  • Replace any easily removable moldy materials like carpets or curtains.
  • Insulate walls or pipes to reduce condensation.
  • Install moisture barriers in basements or under floors if needed.

3. Professional / Extreme Measures

  • Indoor humidity 60% and above
  • Replace rot-damaged boards or cladding.
  • Apply mold-resistant paint or coatings to affected areas.
  • Hire a professional mold remediation company for large infestations.
  • Consider structural changes for long-term moisture control (e.g., improved drainage, exterior waterproofing).
  • In severe cases, temporarily vacate the home during deep remediation.
